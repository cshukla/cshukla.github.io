Welcome


I am Chinmay Shukla, a graduate student in the Biological and Biomedical Sciences program at Harvard University. Outside lab, I am passionate about reading non-fiction, explaining science to people with non-scientific backgrounds and exploring the world.

This page provides information on my research projects. I frequently blog on experiences in graduate school at Outside Two Standard Deviations.
